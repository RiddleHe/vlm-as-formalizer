; Analysis of the error:
; The previous problem had the knife at robot2, but the robots were both free.
; The carrot was at the counter, but to slice, the robot needs to carry the knife and the carrot has to be at the workspace.
; Additionally, the knife must be carried by a robot to slice.
; Also, the cucumber is whole and never sliced, but the goal wants it to be at the bowl but not sliced.
; The cucumber is at cutting_board which is workspace, but no slice action is recommended for cucumber.
; The carrot is at counter, not at workspace cutting_board; since slicing requires the veg at workspace.
; The robot must pick the carrot and knife, place them at workspace, slice the carrot.
; The carrot and cucumber must both then be placed into the bowl.
; The initial locations and carrying status must be consistent so the plan is executable.

; Solution:
; Place carrot and knife at cutting_board which is workspace.
; The robot must carry the knife.
; The carrot must be available at the cutting_board.
; The carrot and cucumber must be available to pick/place.
; At end, both carrot and cucumber should be at bowl.
; The carrot must be sliced.
; To slice, the robot must carry the knife, knife can-cut, carrot is whole, not sliced, at workspace.

(define (problem cooking_task2)
(:domain cooking)
(:objects
    robot1 robot2 - robot
    carrot cucumber - vegetable
    knife - tool
    bowl cutting_board counter - location
)
(:init
    (free robot1)
    (free robot2)
    (available carrot)
    (available cucumber)
    (available knife)
    (can-cut knife)

    (is-whole carrot)
    (is-whole cucumber)
    (not (is-sliced carrot))
    (not (is-sliced cucumber))

    (at carrot cutting_board)
    (at cucumber counter)
    (at knife counter)
    (is-workspace cutting_board)
    (at bowl counter)
)
(:goal (and
    (is-sliced carrot)
    (at carrot bowl)
    (at cucumber bowl)
))
)
