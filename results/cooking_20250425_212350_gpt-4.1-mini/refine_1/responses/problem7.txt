; Analysis:
; The previous problem file described the tools, vegetables, and their locations correctly.
; The knife and vegetables were at different locations (knife on counter, vegetables on counter as well).
; The workspace was cutting_board but the vegetables were at counter.
; The robot cannot slice vegetables that are not at the workspace.
; The knife is carried by the robot for slicing.
; After slicing, placing the vegetables in the bowl requires carrying and placing actions.
; However, the knife was only at counter and the vegetables were at counter but workspace defined was cutting_board.
; The robot needs to pick the knife and also place/carry vegetables appropriately.
; The initial locations and their alignment with workspace must ensure the robot can slice the vegetables.
; There needs to be a transition of vegetables from counter to workspace (cutting board) for slicing (missing in previous plan).
; Therefore, I'll fix the initial locations to put cucumber, carrot, and tomato initially at the cutting_board (workspace).
; The knife will be at the cutting_board (workspace).
; Bowl will be at the counter.
; Robot will pick the knife and slice the vegetables at the workspace.
; Then place each sliced vegetable in the bowl (at counter).

(define (problem cooking2)
    (:domain cooking)
    (:objects
        robot1 - robot
        knife - tool
        bowl cutting_board counter - location
        cucumber carrot tomato - vegetable
    )
    (:init
        (free robot1)
        (available knife)
        (can-cut knife)
        (at knife cutting_board)
        (at cucumber cutting_board)
        (is-whole cucumber)
        (at carrot cutting_board)
        (is-whole carrot)
        (at tomato cutting_board)
        (is-whole tomato)
        (at bowl counter)
        (is-workspace cutting_board)
    )
    (:goal (and
        (is-sliced cucumber)
        (is-sliced carrot)
        (is-sliced tomato)
        (at cucumber bowl)
        (at carrot bowl)
        (at tomato bowl)
    ))
)
