The error in the previous PDDL problem is that the cucumber is initially at the "counter" location, but slicing requires the vegetable to be at an "is-workspace" location (like the "cutting_board"). Also, the knife was initially at the robot, but it should be available at some location to be picked first by the robot. Additionally, the robot must pick the cucumber and the knife first before slicing, and then place the sliced cucumber in the bowl.

To fix these issues:
- Place the cucumber and knife initially at locations, not carried by the robot.
- Place the cucumber at the cutting board (workspace) or require the robot to place it first to the workspace.
- The robot is free at the start.
- Define the goal to have the cucumber sliced and at the bowl.

Here is the corrected PDDL problem:

```lisp
(define (problem cooking2)
  (:domain cooking)
  (:objects
    robot1 - robot
    cucumber - vegetable
    knife - tool
    cutting_board bowl counter - location
  )
  (:init
    (is-whole cucumber)
    (not (is-sliced cucumber))
    (available cucumber)
    (available knife)
    (free robot1)
    (can-cut knife)
    (at cucumber cutting_board)
    (at knife counter)
    (is-workspace cutting_board)
    (at bowl counter)
  )
  (:goal (and
    (is-sliced cucumber)
    (at cucumber bowl)
  ))
)
```
