**Analysis of the Error**

The error "Search stopped without finding a solution" in a planning context means the planner *couldn't* find a sequence of actions from the initial state to the goal state, *given the domain definition*. This doesn't necessarily mean there's a PDDL syntax error. It means the problem is unsolvable with the actions defined in the domain, or that the initial state and goal are too far apart to be reached within the search depth.

Looking at the previous PDDL, the initial state describes three stacks: green on blue, yellow on purple, and orange on red. The goal is to have yellow on orange, red on green, and blue on purple.  The problem is that the initial state has the blocks stacked in a specific order, and the goal requires a completely different order.
