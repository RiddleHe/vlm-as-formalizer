**Analysis:**

**Objects:**
- carrot (whole, on counter)
- bowl (on counter)
- cutting_board (location, next to counter)
- counter (location)
- 2 robots: robot1 (free), robot2 (holding knife)
- knife (held by robot2; cutting tool)

**Initial State:**
- carrot is-whole, available, at counter
- bowl is at counter, available
- cutting_board is-workspace
- knife is cutting tool; at wherever (implicitly held by robot2, not available)
- robot1 is free; robot2 holding knife (not free)

**Goal:**
- carrot is sliced and in the bowl

---

```lisp
(define (problem cook_carrot_bowl)
    (:domain cooking)
    (:objects
        robot1 robot2 - robot
        carrot - vegetable
        knife - tool
        bowl - object
        cutting_board counter - location
    )
    (:init
        (is-whole carrot)
        (available carrot)
        (at carrot counter)
        (available bowl)
        (at bowl counter)
        (can-cut knife)
        (carry robot2 knife)
        (not (free robot2))
        (free robot1)
        (is-workspace cutting_board)
    )
    (:goal (and
        (is-sliced carrot)
        (at carrot bowl)
    ))
)
```
