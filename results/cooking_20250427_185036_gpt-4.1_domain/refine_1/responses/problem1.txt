The error in the previous PDDL problem was that the cucumber and the knife were not in the same location, which is required by the `slice` action (both must be at the same location for slicing to be possible). The knife was on the cutting board and the cucumber was on the counter, so slicing could not occur.

Based on the image, here is the corrected PDDL problem:

(define (problem cooking2)
(:domain cooking)
(:objects
    robot1 - robot
    cucumber1 - vegetable
    knife1 - tool
    bowl1 - object
    cutting_board1 - location
)
(:init
    (at cucumber1 cutting_board1)
    (at knife1 cutting_board1)
    (at bowl1 cutting_board1)
    (is-bowl bowl1)
    (is-knife knife1)
    (is-cucumber cucumber1)
    (clear cucumber1)
    (clear knife1)
    (clear bowl1)
    (empty-hand robot1)
)
(:goal (and
    (sliced cucumber1)
    (in-bowl cucumber1)
))
)
