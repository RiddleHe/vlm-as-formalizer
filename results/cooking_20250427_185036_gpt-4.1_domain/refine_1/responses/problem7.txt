The error in the previous PDDL problem is that all objects (cucumber1, carrot1, tomato1, bowl1) were placed at the same location (table1), but according to the scene from the image:
- The knife is on the cutting board.
- The cucumber, carrot, and tomato are on the table (tray in front).
- The bowl is on the table (tray in front).
- To slice a vegetable, it should be taken to the cutting board, since the knife is there.
- It is also necessary for the robot to move the vegetable to the cutting board before slicing (since slice action requires the vegetable and knife to be at the same location).

Therefore, the previous problem was unsolvable because you could not perform the slice action: the vegetables and knife were not colocated at the start, and objects were not moved to enable it.

Here is the corrected PDDL problem:

```
(define (problem cooking-slice-and-bowl)
(:domain cooking)
(:objects
    robot1 - robot
    cucumber1 - vegetable
    carrot1 - vegetable
    tomato1 - vegetable
    knife1 - tool
    bowl1 - object
    table1 - location
    cutting_board1 - location
)
(:init
    (at cucumber1 table1)
    (at carrot1 table1)
    (at tomato1 table1)
    (at bowl1 table1)
    (at knife1 cutting_board1)
    (clear cucumber1)
    (clear carrot1)
    (clear tomato1)
    (clear knife1)
    (clear bowl1)
    (is-bowl bowl1)
    (is-vegetable cucumber1)
    (is-vegetable carrot1)
    (is-vegetable tomato1)
    (is-knife knife1)
)
(:goal (and
    (sliced cucumber1)
    (sliced carrot1)
    (sliced tomato1)
    (in-bowl cucumber1)
    (in-bowl carrot1)
    (in-bowl tomato1)
))
)
```
