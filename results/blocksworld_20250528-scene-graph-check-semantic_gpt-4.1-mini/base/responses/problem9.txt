The error is a PDDL semantic error related to improperly defining the types of objects in the problem section. The objects such as purple, blue, green, yellow, red, and orange need to be declared with their type (block) for the PDDL parser to recognize them as such. They were listed without "- block" which caused the errors. Also, the predicate (handfull robot1) in the scene graph is inconsistent with (handempty robot1) in the initial state; only one should be present correctly.

Here is the corrected scene graph and PDDL problem:

Scene graph:
block: purple blue green yellow red orange
robot: robot1
(on purple blue)
(on blue green)
(on green yellow)
(on yellow red)
(ontable red)
(ontable orange)
(clear purple)
(clear orange)
(handempty robot1)

PDDL problem:
(define (problem stack_puzzle)
    (:domain blocksworld)
    (:objects
        purple blue green yellow red orange - block
        robot1 - robot
    )
    (:init
        (on purple blue)
        (on blue green)
        (on green yellow)
        (on yellow red)
        (ontable red)
        (ontable orange)
        (clear purple)
        (clear orange)
        (handempty robot1)
    )
    (:goal (and
        (on purple blue)
        (on blue green)
        (on green yellow)
        (on yellow red)
        (on red orange)
    ))
)
