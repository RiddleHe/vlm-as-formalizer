Caption: Two robotic arms are above a cutting board, one robot is holding a knife, and the other robot is free. A whole tomato is on the workspace near the cutting board, and a bowl is placed on the counter ready to receive sliced tomatoes.

PDDL problem:
(define (problem slice_tomato_place_in_bowl)
    (:domain cooking)
    (:objects 
        robot1 - robot
        robot2 - robot
        knife - tool
        tomato - vegetable
        bowl - object
        cutting_board - location
        counter - location
    )
    (:init
        (free robot1)
        (carry robot2 knife)
        (can-cut knife)
        (is-whole tomato)
        (not (is-sliced tomato))
        (at knife cutting_board)
        (at tomato counter)
        (at bowl counter)
        (is-workspace cutting_board)
        (available tomato)
    )
    (:goal
        (and
            (is-sliced tomato)
            (at tomato bowl)
        )
    )
)
