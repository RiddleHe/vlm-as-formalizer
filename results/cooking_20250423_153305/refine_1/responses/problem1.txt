The main error last time was due to malformed PDDL syntax, specifically invalid comments in the `:init` section (`(; Locations is-workspace cutting_board1)` etc.). PDDL does not support inline comments with `;` inside lists—comments must be on their own lines starting with `;`. As a result, objects and initial predicates were not properly specified, leading to parser errors.

  (:domain cooking)
  (:objects
    robot1 robot2 - robot
    cucumber1 - vegetable
    knife1 - tool
    bowl1 cutting_board1 counter - location
  )
  (:init
    (is-workspace cutting_board1)
    (at cucumber1 cutting_board1)
    (at knife1 cutting_board1)
    (at bowl1 counter)
    (is-whole cucumber1)
    (available cucumber1)
    (available knife1)
    (can-cut knife1)
    (free robot1)
    (free robot2)
  )
  (:goal (and
    (is-sliced cucumber1)

  ))
