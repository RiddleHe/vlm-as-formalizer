```pddl
(define (problem blocksworld)
    (:domain blocksworld)
    (:objects

        green - block

        blue - block

            (on red blue)
            (on blue yellow)
            (on yellow purple)

        )
    )

```

3. **Goal State**:

   - The robot is holding each block in the correct order.
   - The blocks are stacked in the desired order: `green` on top of `red`, which is on top of `orange`, which is on top of `blue`, which is on top of `purple`, which is on top of `yellow`.
