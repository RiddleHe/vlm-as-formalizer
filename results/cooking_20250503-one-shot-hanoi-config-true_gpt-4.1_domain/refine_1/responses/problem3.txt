However, robot1 cannot pick the carrot and the knife at different times, because no putdown action exists for objects other than 'place', which requires a location, and placing the knife or carrot does not help facilitate the sequence. There was also a challenge because the carrot and knife were on different locations, further preventing a valid plan.

(define (problem cooking-carrot-bowl)
(:domain cooking)
(:objects
    robot1 robot2 - robot
    carrot1 - vegetable
    knife1 - tool
    bowl1 - object
    table1 cutting_board1 - location
)
(:init
    (at carrot1 cutting_board1)
    (at knife1 cutting_board1)
    (knife knife1)
    (at bowl1 table1)
    (handempty robot1)
    (handempty robot2)
    (free robot1)
    (free robot2)
)
(:goal (and (sliced carrot1) (in-bowl carrot1)))
)
