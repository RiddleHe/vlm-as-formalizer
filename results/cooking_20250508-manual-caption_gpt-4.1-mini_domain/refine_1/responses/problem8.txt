The error in the previous PDDL problem was that the cucumber was initially at the counter, not on the cutting board, and the robot holding the knife couldn't slice it directly unless it is placed on the cutting board first. Also, the tomato was initially on the cutting board but not sliced, which was not addressed properly in the goal. Further, the tomato was placed on the cutting board, meaning it also needs to be sliced and then placed in the bowl.

In the image, the cucumber is on the counter (not the cutting board), the tomatoes are on the cutting board, and the bowl is on the counter. One robot is free; the other is holding the knife. The actions should consider moving the cucumber to the cutting board before slicing it.

Here is the corrected PDDL problem:

```lisp
(define (problem cooking_task2)
(:domain cooking)
(:objects
    robot1 - robot
    robot2 - robot
    cucumber - vegetable
    tomato - vegetable
    knife - tool
    cutting_board - location
    counter - location
    bowl - object
)
(:init
    (at bowl counter)
    (at cucumber counter)
    (at tomato cutting_board)
    (on_cutting_board tomato)
    (free robot1)
    (holding robot2 knife)
    (has_knife robot2 knife)
)
(:goal (and
    (sliced cucumber)
    (sliced tomato)
    (at cucumber bowl)
    (at tomato bowl)
))
)
```
