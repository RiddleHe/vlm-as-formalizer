The error in the previous PDDL problem is that the cucumber was never placed on the cutting_board, so the slice action could not be applied because its precondition requires the vegetable to be on the cutting_board. The goal requires the cucumber to be sliced and placed in the bowl, but the initial state doesn't enable the necessary place action to put the cucumber on the cutting_board first.

To fix this, the cucumber must initially be at a location (counter), and the robot must be free to pick it and place it on the cutting_board before slicing. The bowl and cutting_board are also at the same location (counter) to allow easy manipulation. The robot holding the knife must be at the cutting_board or move to it.

Here is the corrected PDDL problem:

```lisp
(define (problem slice_cucumber)
(:domain cooking)
(:objects
    robot1 robot2 - robot
    cucumber - vegetable
    knife - tool
    bowl - bowl
    counter cutting_board - location
)
(:init
    (at cucumber counter)
    (at bowl counter)
    (at cutting_board counter)
    (free robot1)
    (holding robot2 knife)
    (at robot1 counter)
    (at robot2 cutting_board)
    (not (sliced cucumber))
)
(:goal (and
    (sliced cucumber)
    (at cucumber bowl)
))
)
```
