Analysis of the Error:
The previous problem included the cucumber located on the counter and the knife on the cutting board, while the bowl and tomatoes had specific locations. However, the actions required to solve the task involved slicing the cucumber, which demanded both the cucumber and the knife to be at the same workspace (i.e., the same location with the is-workspace property). But the cucumber was not at a workspace (it was on the counter), so it could not be sliced. Secondly, the bowl was on the counter, while tomatoes were on the cutting board, and thus the robot could not perform actions easily to satisfy the goal due to incompatible initial spatial relationships. Thus, the initial configuration made it impossible to construct a valid plan.

Corrected PDDL Problem:

(define (problem cooking2)
(:domain cooking)
(:objects
    robot1 robot2 - robot
    cucumber1 - vegetable
    tomato1 tomato2 - vegetable
    bowl1 - object
    knife1 - tool
    cutting_board1 counter1 - location
)
(:init
    (available cucumber1)
    (available tomato1)
    (available tomato2)
    (available knife1)
    (available bowl1)
    (free robot1)
    (free robot2)
    (is-whole cucumber1)
    (is-whole tomato1)
    (is-whole tomato2)
    (can-cut knife1)
    (at cucumber1 cutting_board1)
    (at knife1 cutting_board1)
    (at tomato1 cutting_board1)
    (at tomato2 cutting_board1)
    (at bowl1 counter1)
    (is-workspace cutting_board1)
    (is-workspace counter1)
)
(:goal
    (and
        (is-sliced cucumber1)
        (at cucumber1 bowl1)
        (at tomato1 bowl1)
        (at tomato2 bowl1)
    )
)
)
